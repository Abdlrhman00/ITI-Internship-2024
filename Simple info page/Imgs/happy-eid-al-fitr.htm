Eid-al-Fitr 2024 Wishes

Happy Eid-ul-Fitr 2024: Eid-al-Fitr marks the end of Ramadan, the holy month of fasting in Islam. It's a time of happiness and celebration for Muslims all over the world. When the crescent moon is sighted, it signals the end of Ramadan. Families and communities gather to pray, share meals, and exchange greetings. Eid-ul-Fitr is significant as it represents the end of a month-long spiritual journey of fasting, prayers, reflection, and helping others. Muslims fast during Ramadan to honour the first revelation of the Quran to Prophet Muhammad. Eid-al-Fitr is a day of gratitude for the strength and guidance gained during this period of fasting and devotion.
